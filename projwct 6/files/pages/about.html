Our Story

Built by Marketers, For Marketers

Velocity was founded in 2019 by three frustrated marketers who were tired of agencies that prioritized looks over results. We've been on the other side of the table—managing budgets, justifying spend, and desperately needing ROI.

That experience shaped everything we do. We don't chase awards or impressive-sounding metrics. We chase revenue, conversions, and sustainable growth. Because at the end of the day, that's what actually matters.

Today, we're a team of 45+ specialists working with e-commerce brands and SaaS companies across 6 continents. We've generated over $127M in revenue for our clients and helped 350+ brands scale profitably.
