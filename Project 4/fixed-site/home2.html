How It Works

From Newbie to Layout Master

IronRail is structured around the four phases every model railroader passes through. We meet you where you are.

Join Free

Create your profile in 60 seconds. No credit card needed. Access community forums and the layout gallery immediately.

Explore & Learn

Browse thousands of layouts by scale, theme, and era. Read detailed build logs, watch technique videos, and follow top builders.

Share Your Work

Upload your layout photos, write a build log, and get feedback from experienced hobbyists. Every submission is reviewed by our moderators.

Earn Recognition

Get voted Layout of the Month, earn builder badges, and qualify for our annual Iron Rail Awards — the hobby's highest community honor.
