Studio Services

Commission models are scoped by part count, basing complexity, and paint style target. Choose the workflow tier that fits your collection.

Assembly

Cleanup, mold-line removal, pinning, gap filling, and magnetization-ready sub-assemblies.

  • Precision dry-fit checks
  • Transport-safe joins
  • Base prep included

Painting

Layered blends, airbrush transitions, edge highlights, and weathering tuned for your display goals.

  • Faction-consistent palettes
  • OSL and glow effects
  • Tournament durable finish

Full Commission

End-to-end production from unbuilt kit to display-ready hero piece with delivery photos.

  • Concept and moodboard
  • Assembly + painting + base
  • Final review and archive
