About the Studio

Our practice started with narrative skirmish miniatures and grew into a full commission atelier for collectors, studios, and event-level armies. Every project balances tabletop readability with display-level craftsmanship.

The lead artist has over a decade of production painting experience, with specialization in material contrast, atmospheric basing, and cross-faction visual consistency.
