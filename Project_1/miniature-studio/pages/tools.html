Tools or Techs

A professional pipeline combines reliable hardware, calibrated paint chemistry, and repeatable techniques for quality at scale.

Airbrush System

Our dual-action setup allows for hyper-smooth gradients and flawless zenithal priming. This technology is essential for creating the base volumes that bring miniature models to life with atmospheric lighting.

Kolinsky Detail Brushes

We utilize premium natural hair brushes that hold a perfect tip. This enables our artists to execute micro-details like iris reflections and 1mm edge highlights with total surgical precision.

Wet Palette Workflow

Our signature blending technique relies on stable moisture control. The wet palette prevents paint from drying mid-session, allowing for the incredibly thin glazes required for display-level skin tones.

Color-Correct Lighting

We work under 5600K high-CRI lighting to ensure that the color you see on your desk is exactly what was painted in the studio. This eliminates color shift between different environments.

Resin & Plastic Prep Kit

Every commission begins with rigorous preparation. We remove all mold lines and flash, pinned for durability, and gap-filled so that the paint sits on a perfectly smooth and sturdy canvas.

Archival Photo Setup

Your final models are documented in our macro-photography studio. These high-resolution images serve as a permanent record of your collection and are included with every elite-tier commission.

Technique Stack

Surface and Contrast Control

Volumetric mapping, controlled glazes, and directional highlights shape readability at arm's length and macro view.

Material Simulation

NMM-inspired metallic rendering, leather texture, and battle wear provide believable material separation.
