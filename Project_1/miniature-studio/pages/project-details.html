Project Details: Wraith Operative

A full commission Sci-Fi operative focused on reflective stealth armor, visored glow effects, and urban ruin basing.

Project Gallery

Project Summary

Scope

One character model, scenic base, and delivery photography.

Duration

11-day turnaround with two progress checkpoints.

Services

Assembly, paint development, weathering, and matte sealing.

Technical Notes

Palette Architecture

Main armor values used a steel-blue triad with controlled warm accents for focal hierarchy around the helmet and shoulder iconography.

Surface Strategy

Fine sponge chipping and directional grime streaking helped anchor the model in urban terrain while preserving clean silhouette readability.
