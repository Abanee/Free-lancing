ABOUT PROTOWORKS

Pioneering the Future of Manufacturing

Combining cutting-edge technology with expert craftsmanship to deliver precision prototypes and production parts.

OUR MISSION

Accelerating Innovation Through Precision Manufacturing

Founded in 2018, ProtoWorks emerged from a simple belief: that great ideas should never be limited by manufacturing constraints.

Our mission is to democratize access to professional-grade manufacturing, making it faster, more affordable, and more accessible for everyone from solo entrepreneurs to Fortune 500 companies.

JOURNEY

Our Path to 2026

2018

ProtoWorks founded in a small garage with just two FDM printers and a vision for on-demand manufacturing. Initial focus was on rapid prototyping for local inventors.

2020

Expanded to a 5,000 sq ft facility and introduced Industrial SLA tech. Partnered with major automotive firms for rapid prototyping cycles, reducing their R&D time by 40%.

SLA LAUNCH AUTO PARTNERS

2023

Launched SLS production line, unlocking high-complexity parts without supports. Achieved ISO 9001 certification and reached a milestone of 1,000 active global clients.

MILESTONE

10M Parts

2026

The Elite UI Launch. Moving towards AI-optimized material choice and carbon-neutral production labs. Pioneering the future of distributed manufacturing.
