FDM PRINTING

Fused Deposition Modeling

FDM is the most widely used 3D printing technology, perfect for functional prototypes and mechanical testing. Layer-by-layer extrusion creates strong, durable parts with excellent structural integrity.

Available Materials:

PLA ABS PETG TPU Nylon ASA

Best For:

  • Functional prototypes
  • Mechanical parts
  • Large-format printing
  • Cost-effective production

SLA PRINTING

Stereolithography

SLA produces the highest resolution prints with incredibly smooth surface finishes. Perfect for detailed models, jewelry, dental applications, and parts requiring fine features.

Available Resins:

Standard Resin Tough Resin Flexible Resin Castable Resin Dental Resin

Best For:

  • High-detail models
  • Smooth surface finish
  • Complex geometries
  • Jewelry & dental

SLS PRINTING

Selective Laser Sintering

SLS uses powerful lasers to fuse powder materials. No support structures needed, making it ideal for complex geometries and functional end-use parts with excellent mechanical properties.

Available Materials:

PA12 Nylon PA11 Nylon TPU Glass-filled Nylon Alumide

Best For:

  • Production parts
  • Complex assemblies
  • High strength applications
  • No support structures
