Our Process

Every restoration follows a rigorous, transparent methodology

1

Archival Research & Documentation

We begin with deep historical research — original blueprints, period photographs, material specifications, and building records to understand the structure's authentic character.

2

Physical Assessment & Material Analysis

On-site 3D laser scanning, mortar sampling, material testing, and structural evaluation to identify scope and risks before any work begins.

3

Preservation Plan & Client Approval

A comprehensive written preservation plan with phased timeline, material specifications, budget breakdown, and regulatory compliance review — submitted for client approval.

4

Skilled Restoration Work

Certified craftsmen execute the approved plan using authentic methods and sourced materials, with continuous photographic documentation and quality control checks.

5

Final Documentation & Handover

Comprehensive as-built documentation including before/after photography, material records, and maintenance guidelines delivered to the client and regulatory office.
