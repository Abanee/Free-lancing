Our Core Competencies

Specialized services that preserve the past while securing the future

Masonry Restoration

Expert brick and stone restoration using period-appropriate techniques and materials to maintain structural and aesthetic integrity.

  • Historic brick matching
  • Mortar repointing
  • Stone cleaning & repair

Structural Reinforcement

Modern engineering solutions that strengthen historic structures while preserving their original character and appearance.

  • Foundation stabilization
  • Seismic retrofitting
  • Load-bearing analysis

Historical Woodwork

Meticulous restoration of historic wooden elements from ornate moldings to structural timber, preserving craftsmanship.

  • Period millwork reproduction
  • Window restoration
  • Decorative element repair

Facade Preservation

Complete exterior restoration including cleaning, repair, and protective treatments for lasting beauty and durability.

  • Building envelope repair
  • Terracotta restoration
  • Weatherproofing systems

Heritage Consulting

Expert guidance through compliance, grants, and preservation planning to protect and enhance historic properties.

  • Compliance documentation
  • Tax credit assistance
  • Historical research

Systems Integration

Seamlessly integrate modern HVAC, electrical, and plumbing systems while respecting historic fabric.

  • Climate control systems
  • Discreet infrastructure
  • Energy efficiency upgrades

The Restoration Lifecycle

A meticulous, multi-phase approach to ensuring historical longevity

1

Discovery & Audit

Historical research, structural forensics, and material composition analysis.

2

Material Strategy

Custom mortar matching and sourcing period-accurate stone and timber.

3

Elite Intervention

Master craftsmen execute repairs using time-honored techniques.

4

Legacy Validation

Quality assurance, protective treatments, and final heritage certification.
