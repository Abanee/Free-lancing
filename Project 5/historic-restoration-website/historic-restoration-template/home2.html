Technical Preservation Protocol

The Methodology of Time

We don't just repair; we surgically intervene. Our protocol bridges 18th-century craftsmanship with 21st-century material science.

01

Forensic Analysis

Using 3D laser scanning and petrographic testing to identify original material compositions and hidden structural fatigue points.

Phase: Documentation
02

Material Synthesis

Custom-batching mortars and identifying period-correct stone sources to ensure chemical and visual compatibility with the historic fabric.

Phase: Preparation
03

Surgical Intervention

Skilled master craftsmen execute repairs using traditional hand tools, preserving as much original material as structurally possible.

Phase: Execution
04

Seismic Integration

Discreetly integrating modern reinforcement to meet modern safety codes without compromising the building's aesthetic integrity.

Phase: Validation
