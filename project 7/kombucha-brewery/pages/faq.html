🌿 Product & Ingredients
No — our kombucha is completely raw and unpasteurized, which is essential for preserving the live cultures and enzymes. This is what makes it genuinely probiotic. Because of this, all bottles must be kept refrigerated and consumed within 3 months of the production date printed on each bottle.
All fermented beverages contain trace amounts of alcohol as a byproduct of the fermentation process. Our kombucha contains less than 0.5% ABV, which classifies it as a non-alcoholic beverage under US law. Our Wellness Series blends contain even lower levels (< 0.3% ABV). This makes it safe for most people, though we recommend consulting a doctor if you are pregnant or have specific health conditions.
Yes. Every single ingredient we use holds USDA Organic certification. We source our black and green teas from certified organic farms in the Pacific Northwest, and all fruit and botanical additions are verified through our supplier's organic certification program. We never use artificial flavors, colors, sweeteners, or preservatives.
Most of the sugar added during fermentation is consumed by the SCOBY culture. Our finished kombucha contains between 4–9g of sugar per 16oz bottle, depending on the flavor. This is significantly less than commercial sodas (which average 45g+) and even less than many "healthy" fruit juices. Nutritional information is available on each product page.
💚 Health & Wellness
Most nutritionists suggest starting with 4–8oz per day and gradually working up to 16oz if desired. Because kombucha is a living food, it can cause some initial digestive adjustment if you're new to probiotic-rich beverages. Listen to your body and start slowly. For most people, one bottle (16oz) per day is an ideal wellness routine.
We recommend consulting your healthcare provider before consuming kombucha during pregnancy or while breastfeeding. Since it's a raw, unpasteurized product with trace alcohol and caffeine content, individual medical guidance is important. We are not able to provide personal medical advice.
