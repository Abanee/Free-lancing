Serving Suggestions

The Art of the Pour

🍷

Glassware Matters

To appreciate the aroma, pour into a white wine glass or a wide-rimmed tumbler. Swirl gently to release the botanical bouquet.

❄️

Optimal Temperature

Best served at 4–6°C (39–43°F). Too cold and you lose the subtle flavor notes; too warm and the effervescence becomes aggressive.

🥂

Food Pairings

Pair Ginger Turmeric with spicy Thai or Indian cuisine. Original Green Tea is the perfect companion for sushi or fresh spring salads.
