Core Offerings

Everything Included

🧗

IFMGA-Certified Guiding

All lead guides hold IFMGA/UIAGM certification — the highest international standard. Minimum 5 personal 8000m summits each. Guides are selected for technical skill, judgment, and communication.

  • ✓ 1:3 guide-to-client ratio minimum
  • ✓ Dedicated personal Sherpa above C3
  • ✓ Pre-expedition orientation briefings
🏥

Medical & Emergency Care

Every expedition includes a dedicated wilderness medicine physician. Full HAPE and HACE emergency protocols with Gamow bag, dexamethasone, nifedipine, and portable altitude chamber.

  • ✓ Pre-expedition health screening
  • ✓ Daily AMS monitoring protocol
  • ✓ Helicopter evacuation coordination
📡

Communications & Forecasting

Iridium satellite phones, GPS trackers, and base camp WiFi. Premium weather forecasts from MeteoBlue updated every 12 hours. 24/7 radio comms between all camps.

  • ✓ Private summit window forecasting
  • ✓ Family/team satellite updates
  • ✓ Emergency beacon for every client
🎿

Oxygen Systems

Premium Poisk and Summit oxygen with TopOut masks. Minimum 4 bottles per client. Masks and regulators tested and fitted at base camp. Supplemental O₂ available from C3 upward.

  • ✓ Individual mask fitting session
  • ✓ Spare regulators at every camp
  • ✓ Full Sherpa O₂ supply included
🏕️

Camp Setup & Logistics

Full Base Camp setup with mess tent, kitchen tent, communications setup, and individual sleeping tents. All high camps stocked with tents, sleeping bags, stoves, food, and fuel before clients ascend.

  • ✓ BC catering (hot meals 3×/day)
  • ✓ Porters and yaks for load carry
  • ✓ Fixed rope installation
📋

Permits & Documentation

All climbing permits, national park fees, and liaison officer fees included. Visa assistance for Nepal/Pakistan. Full permit paperwork handled by our Kathmandu office.

  • ✓ Nepal/Pakistan climbing permits
  • ✓ Liaison officer arrangement
  • ✓ Customs clearance for gear

Pre-Expedition

Training & Preparation Programme

Six months before your departure, you'll receive a personalised training plan from our head guide. Monthly check-in calls track your progress. Two weeks before departure, mandatory participation in our online pre-expedition briefing.

1
Fitness Baseline Assessment
VO2 max testing, strength benchmarks, cardiovascular endurance baseline
2
Personalised Training Plan
6-month periodized plan from our head guide, adjusted monthly based on progress
3
Gear Review & Briefing
Full equipment review, packing guidance, and technical skills assessment via video call
4
Medical Pre-Screening
Required medical examination with specific high-altitude protocol, reviewed by our expedition medic
