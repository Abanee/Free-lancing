How It Started

A Near-Miss That Changed Everything

In 2007, expedition leader Marcus Reid was guiding a client on Manaslu when a sudden weather shift pinned them at Camp III for 31 hours. They survived — but three climbers from another group with a different operator did not.

The difference wasn't luck. It was the 48 hours of weather data Marcus had studied before departure, the conservative acclimatization schedule that left physical reserves in the tank, and a turnaround plan agreed in writing before the first step above base camp.

Apex Ascent was founded the following year with a single mission: prove that elite guiding and commercial expeditions are not mutually exclusive. The mountain does not care about your ambition. We care about getting you home.

🧗
Marcus Reid
Founder & Lead Expedition Guide

Key Milestones

16 Years of the Summit

2008

Founded in Kathmandu

Marcus Reid and Ang Dorji Sherpa register Apex Ascent. First season: 2 Everest permits, 100% summit rate.

2011

First K2 Expedition

Became one of only four Western guiding companies to successfully summit K2 with paying clients. 4 of 4 climbers reached the top.

2015

Nepal Earthquake Response

Suspended all commercial operations to assist in SAR efforts on Everest. Evacuated 34 climbers from above Base Camp. Zero Apex clients injured.

2019

14 Peak Program Launch

Introduced structured multi-year programs for climbers pursuing all 14 eight-thousanders. 3 clients have now completed the full list with us.

2024

Record Season

22 clients, 22 summits across Everest, Lhotse, Makalu and Cho Oyu. First guiding company to summit all four Nepal 8000ers in a single season.

Core Values

What We Stand For

🛡️

Safety Above Summit

We have turned around 23% of our expeditions before summit due to conditions. Every single climber who turned around is alive. That percentage is a badge of honour, not a failure rate.

🌿

Environmental Stewardship

Every Apex expedition leaves with a certified waste management plan. We remove all human waste from above Camp II. Our Sagarmatha Clean-Up Fund has removed 4.2 tonnes of rubbish from Everest since 2012.

🤝

Sherpa Partnership

Our Sherpa team earn 40% above industry standard wages, receive comprehensive health insurance, and are co-owners of Apex Ascent Nepal Ltd. Their safety matters as much as our clients'.
