Before You Apply

Entry Requirements & FAQ

Minimum requirements: At least one successful 7000m summit (ideally Aconcagua, Denali, or a Himalayan 7000er), documented experience with fixed lines and jumar technique, current high-altitude rescue certificate, and a detailed meeting with our lead guide. We assess each application individually.
Yes. All Apex Ascent expeditions above 8000m include supplemental oxygen as standard. We provide Poisk and Summit oxygen systems, with each climber allocated a minimum of 4 bottles (plus guide and sherpa allocation). We do not support "no O2" attempts as they fall outside our safety protocols.
30% deposit upon acceptance to reserve your spot. 40% due 180 days before departure. Final 30% due 60 days before departure. Cancellations within 60 days: deposit forfeited. Within 30 days: 70% forfeited. We strongly recommend comprehensive cancellation insurance, which we can help arrange.
All expeditions include a weather contingency window (10–15 days depending on route). If no viable summit window opens, we offer a 50% credit toward the following year's expedition. Safety-related turnarounds before summit carry no financial penalty — your life and our reputation are worth more than the optics.
