Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

What does off-grid actually mean?

It means no connection to municipal power grids. Our cabins run entirely on solar panels and battery storage — fully functional but entirely self-sufficient.

Is there WiFi or cell service?

Most properties have little to no cell coverage — that is the point. Some cabins offer Starlink satellite internet for emergencies. Check individual listings for details.

How do I cancel or modify my booking?

Log into your Guest Dashboard to modify dates or cancel. Full refunds are available up to 14 days before check-in. See our cancellation policy for full details.

Do you allow pets?

Several of our cabins are pet-friendly. Filter by "Pets Allowed" on the cabins page, or email us and we will match you with the right property for your furry companion.
