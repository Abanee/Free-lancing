Our Story

Built for those who
need to escape.

We believe the best reset happens far from a signal bar. OffGrid was born to make that possible without sacrificing a warm bed or a hot shower.

Why We Exist

Sustainable stays.
No compromise.

We started OffGrid after noticing a gap: people desperately wanted to unplug, but most "remote" rentals were still tethered to noisy generators, poor construction, or overly precious rules.

Every cabin in our collection is powered entirely by solar, built with care for the surrounding environment, and stocked thoughtfully so you can focus on the silence, not logistics.

What Drives Us

Our Values

Solar First

Every property runs entirely on renewable solar energy with battery backup. No generators, no carbon guilt. Systems are audited and upgraded annually.

Leave No Trace

We are certified Leave No Trace educators. All cabins use composting systems and we partner with local conservation groups to restore surrounding land.

Guest First

Detailed pre-arrival guides, GPS coordinates, emergency contacts, and a 24/7 support line mean you are never truly alone — unless you want to be.
