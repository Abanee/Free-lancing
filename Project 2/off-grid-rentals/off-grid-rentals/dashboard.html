Pine Ridge Cabin

Willamette National Forest, OR

🚗 Getting There
Take I-5 South to exit 194A, then follow Route 22 East for 68 miles. Turn left on Forest Road 2266. Park at the designated lot (GPS: 44.8691, -122.3419). A 0.4-mile trail leads to the cabin. 4WD strongly recommended Oct–April.
🏕️ Local Activities
  • • Marion Lake Trail — 7.8mi loop, moderate difficulty (3mi from cabin)
  • • Opal Creek Wilderness — Old growth forest, swimming holes (12mi)
  • • Detroit Lake — Kayaking & fishing (18mi)
🛒 Nearest Supplies
Closest grocery is Gates Store in Gates, OR (22mi). We strongly recommend stocking up in Salem before heading out. The cabin has a fully stocked spice rack, firewood, and fire starters.
📡 Connectivity
No WiFi (intentional). Verizon gets 1–2 bars at the cabin. A satellite emergency communicator (Garmin inReach) is available on the property for emergencies only.
