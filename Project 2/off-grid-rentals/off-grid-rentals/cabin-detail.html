About this cabin

Perched at 3,400ft elevation in the Willamette National Forest, Pine Ridge Cabin is a fully solar-powered retreat designed for those who want genuine disconnection without giving up comfort.

The cabin sleeps four across two bedrooms, each with handcrafted cedar beds and premium linen. The main living area features a wood-burning stove, reading nooks, and panoramic forest views from floor-to-ceiling windows.

Outside, you'll find a covered fire pit, a wood-fired hot tub, and a composting system that keeps the site perfectly clean. An outdoor solar shower completes the wild experience. Access requires a 0.4 mile forest walk from the designated parking area — all gear transport is by you.
